Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains how AE SafeReport collects, uses, and protects the data submitted through this platform.

Data Collection

We collect the information you voluntarily provide in the report form, including usernames, descriptions, and evidence links.

Data Usage

Submitted data is reviewed only by authorized AE volunteers to assist with extortion cases and community safety.

Data Storage & Security

Data is stored securely on our servers and transmitted via encrypted connections. We do not share your data with third parties outside of AE volunteers.

Your Rights

You may request data deletion or correction by contacting AE moderators. We respect your privacy and comply with relevant laws.

Contact

For questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please reach out to AE support.
